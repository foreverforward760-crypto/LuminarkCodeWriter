## Core Architectural Principles

### 1. **Tumbling Inversion Principle**

The fundamental insight that consciousness and physical stability follow an **inverse relationship** across the 0-9-0 cycle:

| Stage Parity | Physical Stability | Conscious Stability | Implication |
|--------------|-------------------|-------------------|-------------|
| **Even** (0, 2, 4, 6, 8) | High | Low | System is stable externally but unstable internally |
| **Odd** (1, 3, 5, 7, 9) | Low | High | System is unstable externally but coherent internally |

**Implementation in v8.2.1:**
```javascript
const isEvenStage = [0, 2, 4, 6, 8].includes(stage);
const tumblingInversionState = isEvenStage
    ? 'Physically Stable / Consciously Unstable'
    : 'Physically Unstable / Consciously Stable';
```

This flag is now returned with every stage classification, allowing downstream systems to adjust their intervention strategies based on whether they're addressing physical or conscious instability.

### 3. **Arc Direction Inference**

Distinguishes **ascending** vs. **descending** trajectories through NSDT trends:

```javascript
const arcDirection = (recentTensionTrend > 0 && recentAdaptabilityTrend < 0) 
    ? "descending" 
    : "ascending";
```

- **Descending Arc**: Stability increasing, Adaptability holding, Tension low (descent toward Stage 8)
